# Portfolio Positioning

## Executive Narrative

Fischer Product Lab is a portfolio of security, AI governance, trust, and operational risk products that turn complex enterprise controls into usable product experiences.

The Vulnerability Metrics Dashboard shows how vulnerability data can become an executive operating system: clear risk posture, business ownership, SLA pressure, remediation velocity, and leadership decisions.

## Background Alignment

This project maps directly to Trevor Fischer's experience across cybersecurity, technology delivery, product enablement, governance/risk/compliance, AI governance, and enterprise security enablement.

It highlights:

- BISO-style translation between security, technology, and business teams
- Metrics-driven remediation and executive reporting
- Vulnerability management and risk prioritization
- Application security, IAM, infrastructure, and control-oriented thinking
- Consulting-style communication, requirements gathering, and product demos
- Practical delivery enablement across owners and stakeholders

## Interview Talking Points

- "I built this to show how raw vulnerability data can become executive-ready decisions."
- "The dashboard emphasizes business ownership, SLA risk, and remediation commitments instead of only CVSS severity."
- "I used synthetic data and read-only behavior because portfolio demos should be safe by default."
- "The product framing reflects how I would support a CISO office or BISO function: turn messy technical risk into a clear operating rhythm."
- "The same product pattern can extend into TrustDesk, AgentOps, AI vendor governance, and other Fischer Product Lab products."

## Concise Portfolio Story

I built Fischer Product Lab to demonstrate how I productize security and AI governance workflows. The Vulnerability Metrics Dashboard is the first product: an executive-grade remediation command center that helps leaders understand risk concentration, SLA pressure, ownership, and where intervention is needed.
